About Us

Welcome to MindGuard! This mental health chatbot is designed to provide reliable information and resources to help individuals improve their mental health and wellbeing.

We understand that seeking help for mental health concerns can be challenging, and our chatbot is designed to make the process as comfortable and accessible as possible.

However, please remember that our chatbot is not a substitute for professional help. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek help from a mental health professional.

Our Mission

We believe that education and awareness are critical components of mental health. Our chatbot aims to provide accurate and up-to-date information on various mental health conditions, including symptoms, causes and effects. We also provide a variety of resources, such as mental health tests and exercises, to help you evaluate your mental health and improve your overall wellbeing.

Our Team

Chatbot was developed by a dedicated student who is passionate about mental health. The developer is committed to playing a part in breaking down the stigma surrounding mental health and creating a supportive community for individuals to seek help. Thank you for choosing our mental health chatbot as your first step towards a healthier and happier life! We are here to support you every step of the way.
